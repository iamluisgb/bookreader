Questions, answered.

How much does it cost?

The app is free. The agent runs on your own AI key (BYOK): you pay your provider only for what you use — no middleman, no subscription.

Which books work?

Any EPUB or PDF with a text layer: textbooks, technical books, documentation, papers. The agent reads both formats with the same pipeline.

Does it support cloze deletions?

Yes — choose between classic Q&A cards and cloze cards with the key fact hidden, Anki-style.

Is my study material uploaded anywhere?

No. Books live in your browser's storage. When you generate cards, the relevant passages go directly from your browser to the AI provider you configured — and nowhere else.
